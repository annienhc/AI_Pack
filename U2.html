2.2 Why Is AI Sometimes Illogical?

Even if every fact in the AI's response is true, the AI might connect ideas in a way that makes no logical sense.

This is because AI lacks real-world experience and reasoning. It predicts words — it does not actually "think" or understand the physical world.

2.3 Common Logic Problems

There are three main types:

🌍

A. Lacking Real-World Logic

AI misses obvious real-world context that any human would catch

🔀

B. Poor Logical Flow

Ideas are jumbled, disconnected, or don't follow logically

⚔️

C. Contradictory Statements

AI says opposite things within the same text

🔀 B. Poor Logical Flow

A student asked AI to write a product description. Every fact is correct, but the flow is broken:

📝 AI-Generated Text

1. "The QuickDry Pro is a professional hair dryer designed for salon use."

2. "The first electric hair dryer was invented in France in the late 1800s." ⚠️

3. "It uses ionic technology to reduce frizz and dry hair 50% faster."

4. "Therefore, it is the ideal tool for any hairdressing professional."

5. "Order now and enjoy free delivery on all kitchen appliances." ⚠️

Problems: Sentence 2 is about history — irrelevant in a product description. Sentence 5 promotes kitchen appliances in a hair dryer description!

💡 Tip

A well-structured text follows a clear order. If removing a sentence changes nothing, it probably shouldn't be there.

📝 Note to Students

This is an English Language learning pack designed to teach you how to use AI safely while actively improving your own English skills.

You will learn to stop being a "passive user" who just copies text, and become an "active editor" who evaluates, fixes, and adapts AI-generated English.

As you work through this pack, remember:

🤝

AI is your practice partner, not your teacher

AI makes mistakes. Finding those mistakes is exactly how you will practise your English!

💪

Trust your own English

If an AI sentence sounds unnatural, you are probably right. Trust your common sense over the machine.

✍️

You are responsible for the text

You must use your English skills to make sure the text is professional and human!
